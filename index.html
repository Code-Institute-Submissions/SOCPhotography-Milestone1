About Me

Hi, my name is Steve and I'm a PHOTOGRAPHER!
After visiting Tanzania in 2016 I instantly fell in love with the country and everything it has to offer. From the beautiful landscapes of the towns and villages to the amazing wildlife found throughout the country it was hard not to fall in love. I decided to move here in 2018 and since then I have been working closely with the Tanzania Tourist Board helping to promote the country while also working National Geographic and the BBC where you can find my photos published in their magazines and used in a few of their documentaries.

Always wanting to explore and learn more about Tanzania I decided to get a helping hand from
a few natives of the country that share the same passion of photography as me and together we created the "Tanzania Photography Walks Group" where you can join us on weekly walks through various locations. You will get to learn about the areas and also document your walk through photography. We also hold bi-annual trips to some of the many conservation parks throughout the country where you can really get the best wildlife snaps on offer, so make sure and sign up for my newsletter to find out more about our walks and my latest photography.
I hope you enjoy my page and thank you for stopping by.
Asante sana!
