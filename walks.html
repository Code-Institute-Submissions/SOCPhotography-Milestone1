Photography Walks

While speaking with some local tour guides, they told me they were actaully looking to learn more about photography, I then decided to start my own walking group, whether you are a budding photographer trying to learn more or an experienced photographer looking to learn more about Tanzania my walking group is just for you! You can join us on weekly walks through various locations where you will get to learn about the areas and also document your walk through photography. All the latest news about the walks are in my newsletter where you will also find out more about my work and where you can find them in different media throughout the world.

Always wanting to explore and learn more about Tanzania I decided to get a helping hand from a few natives of the country that share the same passion of photography as me and together we created the "Tanzania Photography Walks Group" where you can join us on weekly walks through various locations. You will get to learn about the areas and also document your walk through photography. We also hold bi-annual trips to some of the many conservation parks throughout the country where you can really get the best wildlife snaps on offer, so make sure and sign up for my newsletter to find out more about our walks and my latest photography.
